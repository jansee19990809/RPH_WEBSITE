Philippine Constitution: A History and Evolution from the Revolutionary to Commonwealth Government

  • See, Jan Wilbert
  • December 14, 2020

Philippine Constitution serves as our Law Identity in our country. According to Merriam Webster, it is the nation's or country's written and published basic rules and principles in which the government and people stand for. It also embodies and describes the political and social responsibilities of the people in our country.

Starting from the qualifications and terms of the government officials. And until to the rights and duties of every citizen.

Philippine Constitutions evolves throughout our rich and long history from the Revolutionary Government and up until to the People Power Government.


Malolos Constitution (1899 Philippine Constitution) is the Revolutionary Government Consitution at the time of the colonization of the Spaniard's sparks the fight for the Filipinos freedom and liberty.
